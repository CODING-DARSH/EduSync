Platform Features

EduSync brings together all the tools educators and students need in one seamless, intelligent platform

Course Management

Teachers can create, assign, and manage courses with ease. Track student enrollment, upload materials, and organize classrooms efficiently.

Attendance Tracking

Smart attendance system with daily logs, percentage calculations, analytics, and automated insights for both students and teachers.

AI Risk Prediction

Machine Learning algorithms automatically identify at-risk students using performance, attendance, and behavioral data patterns.

Assignment Management

Students submit assignments online; teachers review, grade, and provide feedback instantly with our streamlined workflow.

Real-Time Notifications

Automated alerts for grades, attendance, risk predictions, and announcements keep everyone informed and engaged.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual dashboards display performance metrics, attendance trends, ML predictions, and comprehensive course statistics.

Role-Based Dashboards

Customized interfaces designed for each user role with relevant tools and information
